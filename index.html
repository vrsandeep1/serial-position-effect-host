Instructions for Serial Position Experiment with Animal names

1. A list of animal names will be displayed in the next page.

2. Try to remember these words visually and mentally.

3. You will have 8 seconds to look at the animal names list and and remember them, after which the screen will automatically move to the next page.

4. A timer as shown on the right will display how much time has passed.

5. When you are ready, click on the START EXPERIMENT button to start the experiment.

6. Once you click on START EXPERIMENT button,you will not be able to return to this page till the experiment is over.
